About

The web we have developed utilizes the power of SSD (Single Shot MultiBox Detector) and YOLOv5 (You Only Look Once) algorithms to estimate the vehicle average in video footage.

The web provides efficient analysis of vehicle averages in various road conditions using state-of-the-art object detection models such as SSD and YOLOv5. By processing video data, the system accurately detects and tracks vehicles, enabling the calculation of average metrics. This tool is invaluable for researchers, transportation authorities, and engineers seeking to understand and optimize vehicle performance across different road scenarios.
